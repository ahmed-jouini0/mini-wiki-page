Messi's records :

  1. Most Ballon d'Or Awards :
    Lionel Messi has won the Ballon d'Or (FIFA World Player of the Year) award multiple times, breaking the record for the most wins. He won it six times as of 2019.


  2. Most Goals in a Calendar Year :
    Messi scored 91 goals for club and country in the calendar year 2012, surpassing the previous record held by Gerd Muller.


  3. Most Goals in a Single European Club Season :
    Messi scored 73 goals for Barcelona during the 2011-2012 season, breaking the previous record held by Gerd Muller.


  4. Most Goals in UEFA Champions League History :
    Messi held the record for the most goals in UEFA Champions League history. He scored over 120 goals in the competition during his time with Barcelona.


  5. :
    Messi scored 14 goals in the 2011-2012 UEFA Champions League season, setting a new record.


  6. Most Hat-Tricks in La Liga :
    Messi holds the record for the most hat-tricks (three goals in a single match) in La Liga.


  7. Most Assists in La Liga :
    Messi also holds the record for the most assists in La Liga history.


  8. Most Goals for a Single Club :
    Messi spent the majority of his career at Barcelona and became the club's all-time leading goal scorer.


  9. Most Goals in El Clásico :
    Messi scored the most goals in the history of El Clásico, the rivalry match between Barcelona and Real Madrid.


  10. Most Consecutive Ballon d'Or Wins :
    Messi won the Ballon d'Or consecutively for four years from 2009 to 2012.


  11. Most Goals in a Single World Cup Tournament :
    In the 2014 FIFA World Cup, Messi scored four goals, which was the highest in that tournament.


  12. Most Goals in a Single Copa America Tournament :
    Messi became the all-time top scorer in Copa America history during the 2021 tournament.
